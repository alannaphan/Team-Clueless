Welcome to our web page!

Our project is a mini database that users can interact with to view animal images.

You can get started by selecting an animal from the list in the sidebar. If there is an animal you happen to really like, you can press the like button at the top right of its page and it will add to its like count, similar to a voting system. You can access our liked animals page by clicking the button at the top right of this page. Then you can see which animal is the most popular!
